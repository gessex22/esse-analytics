Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026  ·  Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Overview

EsseAnalytics ("we", "us", or "our") operates a private content management dashboard at esse-analytics.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account credentials: username and encrypted password for authentication
  • OAuth tokens: access and refresh tokens for connected social media platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram), stored encrypted in our database
  • Content metadata: titles, descriptions, tags, and publishing status of videos managed through the Service
  • Usage data: basic server logs for security and debugging purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively to:

  • Authenticate authorized users and maintain sessions
  • Publish and schedule video content to connected social media platforms on your behalf
  • Retrieve analytics and statistics from connected platforms
  • Maintain the security and integrity of the Service

4. TikTok Data

When you connect your TikTok account, we request access to the following scopes: user.info.basic, video.publish, and video.upload. We use these permissions solely to publish videos on your behalf. We do not share TikTok user data with any third parties. You can revoke access at any time from within the Service or directly from your TikTok account settings.

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in a secured MongoDB database hosted on MongoDB Atlas. OAuth tokens are stored at rest and transmitted over HTTPS only. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. OAuth tokens are deleted immediately upon disconnecting a platform. You may request deletion of all your data by contacting us.

7. Third-Party Platforms

Our Service connects to TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram. Each platform has its own privacy policy that governs their data practices:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Google / YouTube Privacy Policy
  • Instagram Privacy Policy

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, contact us at gesse.socialmedia@gmail.com.
